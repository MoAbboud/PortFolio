# 🎯 TekTak Curation Tips

Quick guide for curating TikTok drama stories like a pro!

## ⏱️ 15-Minute Daily Workflow

1. **Browse Sources (5 min)**
   - TikTok's For You Page
   - Twitter trending topics
   - Reddit: r/TikTokCringe, r/tiktokgossip, r/Deuxmoi
   - Drama news: Dexerto, Drama Alert, Hollywood Unlocked

2. **Select Stories (3 min)**
   - Pick 2-3 juicy stories
   - Prioritize: breaking news > ongoing feuds > old tea
   - Look for high engagement (millions of views/likes)

3. **Add to Admin Panel (7 min)**
   - Open `admin.html`
   - Fill out the form (see tips below)
   - Hit "Add Drama Story"

## ✍️ Writing Great Summaries

### Titles (Keep it punchy!)
- ✅ "Creator Exposed for Fake Pranks"
- ✅ "Influencer Feud Escalates Over Plagiarism"
- ❌ "This Creator Did Something Bad"
- ❌ "Drama Happened Today"

### Descriptions (2-3 sentences max)
**Formula:** What happened + Why it matters + Current status

**Example:**
> "Popular creator caught staging 'authentic' pranks with paid actors. Community demands apology as follower count drops by millions."

- Lead with the drama
- Include consequences/reactions
- Keep it neutral (let readers form opinions)

## 🏷️ Choosing Drama Types

- **Scandal** - Exposed lies, faking content, scams
- **Feud** - Two+ creators fighting publicly
- **Controversy** - Problematic behavior, offensive content
- **Response** - Apologies, statements, clap-backs
- **Legal** - Lawsuits, cease & desists, court cases
- **Drama** - General tea that doesn't fit above

## 📊 Estimating Engagement Stats

Don't have exact numbers? Estimate based on:
- **Viral TikTok:** 5M+ likes, 200K+ comments, 100K+ shares
- **Popular:** 1-3M likes, 50-100K comments, 20-50K shares  
- **Trending:** 500K-1M likes, 20-50K comments, 10-20K shares
- **Notable:** 100-500K likes, 5-20K comments, 2-10K shares

**Pro tip:** Add "~" before estimates (e.g., "~2.4M likes")

## #️⃣ Hashtag Strategy

**Update hashtags weekly, not daily**

**How to find trending hashtags:**
1. Search TikTok for drama-related terms
2. Check what hashtags appear on viral drama videos
3. Look at hashtag view counts

**Include a mix:**
- Evergreen: #DramaAlert, #TeaTime, #Exposed
- Trend-specific: #[CreatorName]IsOver, #[EventName]Drama
- Emotional: #Accountability, #JusticeFor[Name]

## 👑 Featured Creators

**Update monthly**

Look for creators who:
- Consistently cover drama (not just one-off)
- Have 1M+ followers
- Post breaking drama news
- Are respected by the community

**Where to find them:**
- TikTok's drama/tea community
- Twitter drama accounts
- YouTube drama channels with TikTok presence

## 🔄 Content Refresh Schedule

- **Daily:** 2-3 new drama stories
- **Weekly:** Update hashtag trending stats
- **Monthly:** Refresh featured creators list
- **Quarterly:** Archive old drama (delete stories >3 months old)

## 🎨 Quality Over Quantity

Better to post:
- **2 well-written stories** with details
- Than **5 rushed stories** with no context

Users want:
- Clear summaries
- Accurate info
- Relevant drama (not ancient history)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Keep a "Drama Watch List"** - Note interesting stories to write about later
2. **Screenshot receipts** - Save screenshots in case videos get deleted
3. **Check timestamps** - Only post recent drama (within last 48 hours ideally)
4. **Verify before posting** - Make sure drama is real (lots of fake tea out there)
5. **Stay neutral** - You're a curator, not a judge
6. **Update stories** - If drama evolves (apology issued, lawsuit filed), add new entry

## 📱 Mobile Curation

The admin panel works on phones! You can:
- Curate during your commute
- Add stories while browsing TikTok
- Update on the go

Just bookmark `admin.html` on your phone.

## 🚫 What NOT to Post

- Private/doxxing info (addresses, phone numbers)
- Unverified rumors with no source
- Drama involving minors (under 18)
- Content that violates TOS (hate speech, etc.)
- Your personal opinions (stay objective)

## 🎓 Leveling Up

Once you're comfortable:
1. Develop your "voice" in summaries
2. Add context for viewers out of the loop
3. Create "Drama of the Week" roundups
4. Theme days (e.g., "Throwback Drama Thursdays")

---

**Remember:** You're helping people stay informed without the endless scroll. Quality curation is a service! 🍿
